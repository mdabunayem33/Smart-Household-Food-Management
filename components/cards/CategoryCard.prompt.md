Large square tap card for category/product pickers — Add Food step 1 (Choose Category) and step 2
(Choose Product). Renders a flat vector `FoodIllustration` on a tinted surface when `illustration` is
given (preferred); falls back to a filled Material Symbol if not.

```jsx
<CategoryCard label="Vegetables" illustration="vegetables" tone="green" />
<CategoryCard label="Frozen" icon="ac_unit" tone="blue" selected />
```
