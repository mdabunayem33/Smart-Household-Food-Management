Richer flat-vector "scene" illustration for onboarding — a phone + person/family + food composition
per beat, in the same rounded soft-gradient shape language as `FoodIllustration`. Human figures are
abstract silhouettes (no facial detail) to stay clean and premium rather than cartoonish.

```jsx
<OnboardingScene type="track" />
<OnboardingScene type="reminders" size={240} />
<OnboardingScene type="shopping" />
```

3 keys: `track` (person adding items to a smart pantry/phone inventory), `reminders` (phone with
green/yellow/red freshness notification cards + bell), `shopping` (family with cart + checklist card).
