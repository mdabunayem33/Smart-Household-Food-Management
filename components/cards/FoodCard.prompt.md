Inventory row card — flat vector food illustration thumbnail (falls back to a filled icon tile), name,
quantity, freshness bar. Supports swipe-left-to-delete / swipe-right-to-edit at the screen level (see
ui_kits Inventory screen).

```jsx
<FoodCard name="Tomato" quantity="6 pcs" illustration="tomato" freshness="soon" expiryLabel="Expires in 2 days" />
```
